## F Keys on Mac keyboards - (fn)


F1  - Decrease display brightness
F2  - Increase display brightness

F3  - Open Mission Control
F4  - Open Launchpad

F5  - Decrease keyboard brightness
F6  - Increase keyboard brightness

F7  - Skip back (Audio)
F8  - Pause / Play (Audio)
F9  - Skip forward (Audio)

F10 - Mute
F11 - Volume down
F12 - Volume Up
